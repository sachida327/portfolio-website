The Full Stack Developer: An Overview Before we delve into Sachidanand's journey, let's define what it means to be a full stack developer. A full stack developer is someone who possesses expertise in both the front-end and back-end development aspects of web applications. In simple terms, they can work on the client side (what users see) and the server side (how data is managed) of an application. They are like the Swiss Army knives of the tech world, capable of handling a wide range of tasks and challenges.
